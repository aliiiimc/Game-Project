# Cards

## Character

- Miner: fast objective unit, high movement range, low damage, medium-low HP, cannot attack on the turn it is spawned
- UFO Cow: anti-resource unit, low damage against units/buildings, bonus damage against Field/resource buildings
- European King: colonizes opponent building and dies, low stats, boosts troops
- Knight: avg stats
- Archer: avg stats, long range
- Spearman: anti-melee unit, good against tanky/frontline characters, average HP
- Bomber: high damage unit,  becomes weak after dropping bomb, good against buildings
- Engineer: support unit, repairs allied buildings
- Priest: support unit, heals allied characters, weak combat stats
- Dragon: flying unit, cannot be hit by melee units like Knight or Spearman, can be hit by ranged/projectile attacks like Archer, Watch Tower, and anti-air buildings


## WorldEffect

Visible effect on the board. Placed on tile(s), can have a sprite, zone, or duration.

- Field: accelerates money generation
- Mines alert: moderate ATK
- Camp: force new spawn location
- Wall : to prevent enemy from passing or attacking
- Watch Tower: attacks enemy units in range
- Hospital: heals allied characters nearby
- Fog: gives 2 turn half tile movement to the enemy characters on the tile 
- Anti-Air Tower: building made to counter flying units like Dragon


## Spell

No visible board object. Applies an instant or temporary effect by modifying stats, resources, or state.

- Revival: revives  character dead from last round
- +2 Speed: adds 2 movement capacity, doesn't work on miner.
- Freeze: enemy unit cannot move for 1 turn
- Tax Collection: choose field of enemy player and takes away the money generated of the field and gives it to you
- Sabotage: disables enemy building for 1 turn
- Lightning Strike: small direct damage, can target Fort
